How to service?

This application is used to reduce the food-waste and make a service for the people who are involved in hunger. I called Get-foods. Get-foods can provide a platform for people who have extra and fresh foods to store in the specific location instead of regarding it as rubbish. In this situation, people who suffer from hunger can get those foods for free. For foods-provider, they are able to find the nearest foods-keeping box by this application, in order to maximize convenient. For consumers, they can see all the foods which contain in foods-keeping box. If consumer want it, just go and get it.
